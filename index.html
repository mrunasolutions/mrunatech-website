About Us

About Mruna Technologies

MRUNA symbolizes growth, transformation, and purposeful innovation—helping organizations convert business challenges into measurable technology outcomes.

Mruna Technologies Private Limited traces its origins to 2022, when consulting operations commenced under TechInsights IT Solutions. The business later evolved into Mruna Solutions and subsequently into Mruna Technologies Private Limited, reflecting a broader vision for enterprise technology, digital transformation, and innovation.

Today, Mruna Technologies is a Hyderabad-based technology consulting and advisory company focused on Enterprise Architecture, Oracle ERP, Digital Transformation, Cloud, Artificial Intelligence, Application Managed Services (AMS), Technology Governance, and Business Advisory Services.

Founded by industry professionals with over 22 years of global experience, Mruna Technologies combines deep business understanding with strong technology expertise to deliver practical, scalable, and outcome-driven solutions.
